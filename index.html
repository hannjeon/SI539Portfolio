I do what I love

WORK

Research

I believe that good design cannot be divorced from the user research process. Understanding a user is essential in the human-centered design process. I listen to users openly and with empathy, and I try to discover their real needs.

Design

I am involved in all parts of the design process: concept development, information architecture, wireframing, interaction design, UI design, prototyping, and usability testing. I specialize in translating concepts into digital products, services, and experiences through an iterative design process.

Build

I design and build working interactive prototypes to test, explain, and explore ideas. My proficiency in design tools such as Adobe Creative Suites and my coding skills enable me to build different levels of fidelity. This website is also designed and coded by myself.
